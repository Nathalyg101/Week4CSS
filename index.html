Nathaly's Biography


Who I am as a Designer

Design is about imagining, exploring, creative thinking, and being able to excute those ideas. The aspect I enjoy about design, is not being confined to a certain measurment of it. I don't solely have to work in a specific area for the rest of my life but instead can enjoy a little bit of everything because we work as a team to move foward in the world of design.

Who I am as a Person

I enjoy very few things, but those few things are what I like to keep close to my heart. I enjoy colllecting little trinkets like funko pops, music albums, and plushies. I also love to walk around NYC, it's one of the few things I can do freely without needing to waste money, I can go to open spaces and take a breath of fresh air and feel as all my stresses lift off my shoulders. While doodling in my free time, I also enjoy watching movies like Avatar, Avengers Endgame, and other fantasy or romance movies.

Concentrations

In my area of study; communications design, I focus on branding, packaging, and advertising design. Although I thoroughly enjoy some of the other fields, I really zero in on these three areas, as I feel myself lose track of time and enjoy the process of creating. I also have a background in illustration as I've been illustrating for about 5-6 years now, and love to implement some illustrations in my designs. I want to be able to bring both my skills and my way of thinking to the next level and continue to grow as an individual.

What I hope to acheive

I hope to captivate the attention of my target audience with my work in accordance to the project. Be able to successfully captivate them and estabslish the concept and creative thinking that was devoted to the making of the final executations clearly and consise. While also making them intrigued and critically think about the meaning revolving such works.
